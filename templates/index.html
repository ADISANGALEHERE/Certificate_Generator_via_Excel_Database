Vision and Goals of NSS

Vision

The vision of NSS is to develop a sense of social responsibility and awareness in students through various social services and community development programs.

Goals
  • To understand the community in which they live and work.
  • To understand themselves in relation to their community.
  • To develop capacity to meet the needs and the problems of the community in a socially responsible manner.
  • To engage in activities that enhance the personal and collective development of the volunteers.
